Ecology, reimagined

ECOnnY is a team reimagine the way of interaction between humans and nature. Ecology (ECO) and Fun (nnY) are the core values which we consistently insist on. We believe we can help the nature voice their opinions through hardware, data, artificial intelligence and social networks. We believe we are changing the way we communicate with the nature around us.
How can we best innovate new plant interactions for the 21st century?
At ECOnnY, we are cultivating the next generation of plant interactions for improving our indoor environment and bring us more fun in our daily life.

Bridge the gap from 5 parts
Reimagined the ecology from 5 parts. Cool Hardware, top data analyse, artificial Intelligence，funny data visualization and social network. We are trying to bridge the nature communication gap through the latest technologies.
6 programs, we lead step by step
There are 6 programs in progress. Life Online Box, surprise Life, green pet app, cultivate Love, say hi to your parents and green wall. We are cultivating the next generation of plant interactions step by step.
